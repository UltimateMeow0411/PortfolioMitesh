About Orangutan

A modern esports brand shaped by ambition, culture, and community.

Orangutan is built to be memorable on stage and meaningful off it, with a fan experience that feels energetic, premium, and close to the people who support the brand.

Our Story

Created to stand out in a fast-moving esports scene.

Orangutan was formed from a vision to build an esports organization that feels competitive, stylish, and deeply connected to its audience. The goal is not only to field strong teams but to shape a larger identity that fans want to wear, follow, and believe in.

With bold visuals and an ambitious mindset, Orangutan continues building a space where players, creators, and supporters all move together.
